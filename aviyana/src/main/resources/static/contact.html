Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our products and services

How do I know if your products are authentic?

All our products are sourced directly from authentic spiritual centers, temples, and certified artisans. We provide certificates of authenticity and detailed information about the origin of each product.

Do you offer spiritual consultations online?

Yes, we offer both in-person and online spiritual consultations via video calls. Our experienced spiritual guides are available for personalized sessions to help you on your spiritual journey.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for most products. However, certain spiritual items that have been blessed or energized cannot be returned for spiritual reasons. Please check individual product pages for specific return policies.

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping takes 3-7 business days within India. Express shipping is available for 1-3 business days. International shipping takes 7-14 business days depending on the destination.

Can I track my order?

Yes, once your order is shipped, you'll receive a tracking number via email and SMS. You can track your order status in real-time through our website or the courier partner's website.
